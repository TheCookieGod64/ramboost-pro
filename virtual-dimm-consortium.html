About the Consortium

Founded in 2021, the Virtual DIMM Consortium (VDC) is a non-profit international standards organization dedicated to standardizing software-to-silicon interfaces. Our members include major tech companies, imaginary hardware suppliers, and top-tier cloud RAM innovators.

By establishing universal specifications, VDC ensures that downloaded RAM from verified suppliers (like RAMBoost Pro™) is recognized as actual physical hardware by your operating system, preventing hardware conflicts and BIOS failure loops.
